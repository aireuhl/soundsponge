the questions
do sea sponges have ears?
do they hear collectively in their colonies or as the individual?
what sounds do these multicellular organisms recognize when a nudibranch preys upon their neighbor?
what relation(s) do we (as humans) share with the ocean and her residents? are we becoming in-kind, living alongside, making kin, or simply with?
how do we reconcile the impossibility of * knowing * the experience of these creatures ((we may never know what it is to * be bat * or * think coyote* )) with what we are only nearly beginning to understand as anthropocentric?
    what lasting impact do we make by attempting …
  • cognition / reason / knowledge
  • inquiry /observation / perception
  • absorbtion / alteration / transduction
